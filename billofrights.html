Bill of Rights

These posters are meant to be a remake of the Bill of Rights in a larger format. The top poster is contemporary and represents a facade, using the large mass of black to hide the words, representing the government and their actions hiding the complete truth from the public. The bottom is a traditional style of the poster using typefaces and layout that represents a more toned down version fitting that of the actual Bill of Rights.

These are some two of the roughs I created that lead up to the final products of the posters.
