This is a small book I worked on in my Publication class. Based on what was known as the start of the Cthulu mythology in books, I used this idea to create my cover for the book. Although, the creature was never stated, I used descriptions from the book to conceptualize the creature. The typesetting of the book uses typefaces from the era the book was created, Alegreya, and Copperplate Gothic as one that alludes to the theme of the book.
